Untuk tugas IPS, kami harus mengobservasi tentang National Palace Museum

The National Palace Museum in Taipei, Taiwan, holds one of the world’s largest collections of Chinese itmes. Having over 700,000 pieces that cycles every 6 months to 2 years, it showcases treasures from thousands of years of Chinese history, including ancient jade, bronze, calligraphy, paintings, ceramics, and rare books. Famous items like the Jadeite Cabbage and Meat-Shaped Stone are visitor favorites. Originally from Beijing’s Forbidden City, these artifacts were moved to Taiwan to protect them. Today, the museum is a top destination for learning about Chinese culture and history.
